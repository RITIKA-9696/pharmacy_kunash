Exchange Policy

Non-Returnable Items

  • All prescription medicines
  • OTC medicines, vitamins, and supplements
  • Refrigerated or temperature-sensitive items
  • Opened or used products

Eligible for Exchange

  • Damaged, leaked, or expired products (with photo proof)
  • Wrong medicine dispatched
  • Missing items in order

Must be reported within 24 hours of delivery.
