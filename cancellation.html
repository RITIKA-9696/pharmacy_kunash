Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your order only before 30 minutes of placing order.

Cancellation Window

  • Within 30 minutes of placing order.
  • After pharmacist verification → No Return for Any product
  • After 30 minutes of order placed → Cancellation window closed

How to Cancel

  1. Go to My Orders
  2. Select the order
  3. Click "Cancel Order" (only allowed within 30 mins of placing order)
  4. Or call us at +91-7020799618
