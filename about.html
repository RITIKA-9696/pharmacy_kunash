About Dr. Gandhi:

Dr. Alok Gandhi is committed to delivering compassionate, patient-centered care combined with clinical excellence. He specializes in advanced obstetric and gynecological procedures, including laparoscopy and sonography, ensuring accurate diagnosis and optimal outcomes.

He actively participates in seminars, workshops, and medical conferences to stay updated with the latest advancements in reproductive medicine. Dr. Gandhi's approach emphasizes evidence-based practice, personalized care, and holistic support, helping patients navigate even the most complex pregnancies with confidence and safety.

Dr. Shilpa Ghodke Gandhi

Consultant Surgeon

Kanakvandana Maternity & Surgical Hospital, Phaltan

As the only female surgeon in the Phaltan area, Dr. Shilpa stands as an inspiring example of dedication and excellence in a field often dominated by men. Her empathetic nature and evidence-based approach have earned her the trust of countless patients and families.

Specializations:

  • Anorectal Diseases (Fistula-in-ano, Piles, Fissures)
  • Non-surgical Treatment of Kidney Stones
  • Laser and Cosmetic Surgery
  • Garbhsanskar Coaching for Expecting Parents
  • Therapeutic Yoga for PCOS, Infertility, and Pregnancy

Dr. Shilpa is best known for treating the root cause of ailments rather than rushing into surgical interventions. She has extensive experience in managing anorectal diseases such as fistula-in-ano, piles, and fissures, with a strong focus on avoiding unnecessary surgeries. She also specializes in non-surgical treatment of kidney stones and is trained in laser and cosmetic surgery, offering modern, minimally invasive options to her patients.

A believer in integrated medicine, Dr. Shilpa combines medical science with holistic healing. As a certified Garbhsanskar Coach, she guides expecting parents on prenatal practices that support the baby's mental and physical development in the womb. Her expertise in therapeutic yoga for PCOS, infertility, and pregnancy helps women achieve balance, strength, and wellness naturally. She also conducts personal yoga sessions for couples facing infertility, promoting physical and emotional harmony.

Vision:

To avoid unnecessary surgeries by focusing on holistic and preventive care, to treat every patient with empathy, and to spread awareness that surgery is not something to fear—but a solution only when truly needed.
